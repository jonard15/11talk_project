About 11talk
11Talk is an Online English school run by the Philippines' top language school, Pines International Academy. You may learn English online from any device like a computer, smartphone, or tablet as long as you have a stable internet connection.

11talk has a curriculum team that designs lesson materials for all skill levels. Our lesson materials cover a wide range of topics to allow students to choose lessons that match their interests. The team has made sure that the lesson materials are in par with the CEFR leveling system.

Account registration

Students need to sign up for an 11talk account. After that, they could avail of 1 trial class and free English level assessment.

11talk registration only takes a few minutes. To start, go to pinestalking.com and follow these steps

1. Fill out the registration form and provide your trial class information like the desired time, course, and your contact details
2. Upon completing the registration, you should receive a confirmation email; click on the link in the email to confirm your email address.
3. Click the register button to start using 11talk

Can I take my English class while staying abroad?
The English video call program for 11talk students provides a sound class environment through Zoom

【Attending a course using homepage】
  • 1. Log in to the homepage (pinestalking.com)
  • 2. Click "Enter the class" on your class date
  • 3. Click "OK" For the installation of our video class program.
  • 4. Automatically enter class/ start class
【Attending a course using Skype】
  • 1.Log in to Skype 5 minutes before the class begins.
  • 2. Accept the Skype contact request from the teacher.
  • 3. If the call comes from your teacher, reply/start the class.
*Things to prepare for the class*
  • using a laptop or computer
    • Webcam and headset with microphone
  • using a smartphone
    • Earphone with microphone

What happens to my class if I cannot attend classes due to my circumstances?
If you are unable to attend classes due to your circumstances, you can cancel your class by using the class reservation system from our home page.

You can also send a message or contact the consultant so you can arrange for a make up class. Makeup class is available within the course duration.
