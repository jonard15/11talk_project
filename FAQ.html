Frequently Asked Questions

What time is the available class hours ?
Class hours is from 7:00 am to 23:00 (PH time) on weekdays.
Does the class start on the first day of every month?
Classes can be taken from any date the student wants to take except weekends.
What is a trial class?
The trial class is free for you to experience 11talk class before class registration.
Classes are held for 20 minutes, and you can receive feedback from your teacher after class. You can apply for the trial class at the bottom of the homepage.
What do I need to attend classes?

Prepare a headset with built-in microphone and a webcam.

*For laptop users, prepare a headset with a microphone if your laptop already has a built-in camera

*For cell phone users, prepare earphone with built-in microphone that you use for the call.

What payment methods can I use for my class registration?

You can pay by 1) bank transfer or 2) Credit card.

When you apply for the courses, inform the consultant about the payment method. The consultant will also give other necessary information about fees.

What is more recommended, English audio class or video class?

English video class provides better English learning experience as students could check the expressions of the teachers through facial and mouth movement , so they can improve their participation and understanding. For more effective classes at 11talk, all classes shall be done using video calls.
