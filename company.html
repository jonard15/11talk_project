We are 11talk

11Talk is an Online English school run by the Philippines' top language school, Pines International Academy. It stands for online one on one learning, and we have proven that one on one English learning is the best method for language development.

We build a foundation for your English skills at your most convenient time. We have been in the English Education industry for 20 years. The premium quality education provided by our certified language teachers makes us the best choice for online English learning.
