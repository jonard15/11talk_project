Systematic and Effective Learning Method

11talk's 4-step learning method has created great results in improving students' English macro skills from listening, writing, and reading, and speaking.

1 Free Class & English Level Test

Step 1 (Preview)

Learn vocabulary and expressions before the class through initial quizzes, English audio files, and follow-up conversations.

Script

You may start practicing the new expression and words for your incoming lesson by listening to the audio script.

Quiz A

Take Quiz A and match the right word / phrase that will complete the sentence

Quiz B

Remember the new expressions by taking quiz B and re-arranging the words

Mimic

One of the best ways of English Learning is mimicking. it will be easier for you to remember the words and expressions by mimicking audio script with proper pronunciation

Dictation

retention is more effective by answering the dictation exercise.

Result

Check the things you have missed from your lesson preview and make sure to remember the new words and expressions from the unit

Step 2 (1 on 1 class with your teacher)

Learn vocabulary and expressions before the class through initial quizzes, English audio files, and follow-up conversations.

Reading

Practice accent and pronunciation with your 1:1 Teacher by reading the lesson script.

Expression/Patterns

Learn practical expressions/patterns and similar expressions for everyday use

Role-Play

Engage in activity with your teacher through the role play section

Discussion

Gain fluency by using the practical expressions and patterns in answering your teacher’s questions.

Result

Seal in what you have learned by using it on your written output.

Step 3 (Homework)

Give in to more practice by completing your homework and using the learned expressions and vocabulary set on your written output.

Homework

Write using the expression and vocabulary you learn.Write about 200 to 1000 characters
